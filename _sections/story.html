Our Story

The “Chance” band began its journey in 1996. Through their music they led many hearts to Christ. They use their lyrics to inspire the young generation of today’s times. Each and every one of the group member’s dedicated their hearts and souls to God. They formed an alliance between one another and brought their talents together to form something bigger than just a band but a brotherly friendship. Take the Chance and listen.
